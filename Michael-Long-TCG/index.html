See my vision for the Pokémon TCG and PTCG Live

I believe PTCG Live should be the best place for Pokémon TCG online, uniting players worldwide and catering to all skill levels. Drawing from my experience as a PTCG Live user and insights gathered from discussions with prominent players, here are potential projects to enhance the platform:

Game Vault

Rewatch and save past matches! Users could analyze their gameplay and share their favorite moments with friends. The vault could also feature famous matches from the past accompanied by expert commentary.

Advantages: Increased user engagement by offering a platform for sharing and learning from others' experiences. This feature could complement the forthcoming battle log: users could simply click on a log to immediately locate and relive pivotal moments in their games.

Implementation: Developing a method for saving + accessing matches, with an initial focus on uploading famous games, such as the 2015 US National Finals and the 2014 World Championships Finals, to captivate users and showcase the vault's functionality.

Personal Trainer

Introducing your personal AI trainer—an invaluable companion to navigate strategies, explore popular decks, and analyze your gameplay!

Advantages: This feature would cater to both novice players seeking guidance and seasoned players aiming to elevate their gameplay. It also directly synergizes with the Game Vault, empowering players to analyze past games and pinpoint areas for improvement.

Implementation: Leveraging PTCG Live's existing infrastructure to gather data from high-level matches and initially deploying simple game heuristics for decision-making. As we progress and collect more match data, adding advanced technologies like neural networks will further enhance the AI trainer's capabilities.

Ladder Leaderboards

Promote the competitive experience with monthly leaderboards, showcasing the top-ranked players, their current decks, and the option to spectate their matches in real-time!

Advantages: Fosters a thriving competitive ecosystem by incentivizing active participation through a ranking system. It serves as a beacon for players seeking inspiration, allowing them to explore the decks used by top-performing players. The addition of a spectate option offers entertainment and educational value as players watch top gameplay.

More Personal Stats

Enable users to track their progress through battle history, deck records, and all-time stats such as highest ranking, cards drawn, damage dealt, booster packs opened, and more.

Advantages: This feature offers insights into players' Pokémon TCG journey, showcasing their achievements, milestones, and encounters with fellow players. It serves as a personalized archive of their growth and experiences within the game, fostering a deeper connection with the Pokémon TCG community.

...and many other ideas. I'm happy to provide more color on a call!
